Using

Once connected, a variety of screens and control modes can be chosen from to operate the model car.

Controlling the Car

Several control schemes exist for moving the model car and can be selected by clicking the three dots on the top right of the screen on the menu bar.

Random Walk

In this mode, the car drives until nearing an obstacle and then turns a random amount and continues driving.

Joystick Control

In this mode the car can be controlled with the joystick on the bottom right of the screen.

Tilt Control

In this mode, on devices with accelerometers, the car can be controlled by tilting the android device. Tilt forward or backwards to drive forward or reverse and left or right to turn. Tapping the joystick will recalibrate the tilt sensor.

Simple Waypoint Control

In this mode, the car will follow the trail marked by the placed Waypoints. See the LaserScan view for placing and viewing Waypoints.

Waypoint Control

Similar to Simple Waypoint Control, in this mode the car uses a potential field calculated from it's laser scanner to navigate around obstacles.

Navigation Drawer Views

Once connected to the model car, the navigation drawer on the left can be opened to choose from the following screens:

The Overview screen

The Overview screen contains both the Camera screen and the Car screen.

The Camera screen

The Camera screen shows the view from the car's camera.

The Car screen

The Car screen option shows obstacles sensed by the car's Kinect. The blue arrow indicates the car's current direction in relation to the scan data.

This screen also shows the current Waypoint trail used by the various Waypoint control modes. Waypoints can be dropped or deleted with a double tap and moved by selecting them via a long press. Dropping a Waypoint on the existing trail will insert it into the trail.

Preferences

The Preferences allows you to edit a variety of settings including the ROS topics you are connected to and warning options.
