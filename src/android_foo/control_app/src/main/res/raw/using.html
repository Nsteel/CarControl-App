Using

Once connected, a variety of screens and control modes can be chosen from to operate the model car.

Controlling the Car

Several control schemes exist for moving the model car and can be selected by clicking the drop-down menu on the top right of the screen.

Remote Control

In this mode the car can be controlled with the joystick on the bottom right of the screen.

Navigation Drawer Views

Once connected to the model car, the navigation drawer on the left can be opened to choose from the following screens:

The Overview screen

The Overview screen contains both the Camera screen and the Car screen.

The Camera screen

The Camera screen shows the view from the car's camera.

The Car screen

The Car screen option shows obstacles sensed by the car's Kinect. The blue arrow indicates the car's current direction in relation to the scan data.

Telemetry

The Telemetry screen option shows all the sensor and odometry data from the car you are connected to.

Preferences

The Preferences allows you to edit a variety of settings including the ROS topics you are connected to.
